# /accessibility-ch — Swiss Accessibility Standards

## Legal Framework

| Level | Instrument | Scope |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Federal | **nLHin** (Behindertengleichstellungsgesetz / LHand) | Legal obligation for public buildings, services |
| Federal technical | **SIA 500** (Hindernisfreies Bauen, 2009) | Technical standard — the implementation norm |
| Cantonal | Cantonal building laws | Incorporate SIA 500 requirements |

**nLHin applies to:**
- Public buildings and facilities
- Buildings with ≥50 workplaces
- Buildings with ≥50 housing units (common areas)
- Renovation of public-facing parts

**SIA 500 applies to** (cantonal integration):
- New construction: mandatory for most building types
- Renovation: apply "as far as technically possible and economically reasonable"

## Key Dimensions (SIA 500)

### Circulation

| Element | Minimum dimension |
|---------|------------------|
| Corridor width (accessible) | 120 cm clear |
| Corridor width (wheelchair passing) | 150 cm |
| Doorway clear width | 80 cm |
| Doorway clear width (recommended) | 90 cm |
| Turning radius (wheelchair) | 150 cm diameter |
| Ramp gradient | max 6% (1:16) |
| Ramp gradient (short ramps <2m) | max 12% (1:8) |
| Ramp width | min 120 cm |
| Landing at ramp top/bottom | 150 × 150 cm |

### Entrance

- Level access (max 2.5 cm threshold) or ramp
- Automatic or easy-open door preferred
- **Tactile guidance strips** at main entrances (public buildings)
- Minimum clearance beside door handle: 60 cm (approach side)

### Lifts

| Parameter | Standard | Wheelchair accessible |
|-----------|---------|----------------------|
| Cabin depth | 110 cm | 140 cm |
| Cabin width | 90 cm | 110 cm |
| Door clear width | 80 cm | 90 cm |
| Landing depth | 150 cm | 150 cm |

### Accessible WC

Minimum: 165 × 165 cm clear floor area
- Transfer space: 90 cm at both sides of WC (or one side for half-accessible)
- WC seat height: 46–48 cm
- Grab rails: fold-down on transfer side, fixed on wall side
- Washbasin: accessible below (knee clearance), controls operable with fist
- Mirror: bottom edge ≤ 90 cm

### Parking

- 1 accessible space per 50 standard spaces (minimum)
- Dimensions: 350 × 500 cm (with lateral transfer zone 150 cm)
- Location: closest to building entrance

## Building Types — Obligations Summary

| Building type | Accessible entrance | Lift | Accessible WC |
|---------------|---------------------|------|---------------|
| Public building / offices | Mandatory | Mandatory (>1 floor) | Mandatory |
| Retail | Mandatory | Recommended | Recommended |
| Multi-family >8 units | Common areas | If >3 floors | Common areas |
| Hotels | Required % of rooms | Mandatory | Mandatory |
| Industrial / warehouse | Workplaces accessible | If multi-floor | Mandatory |

## Renovation — Proportionality Test

For renovation, accessibility applies when:
- Cost of accessibility measures ≤ 5% of total renovation cost (nLHin threshold)
- Technically feasible without disproportionate structural intervention

Document proportionality assessment in permit application if claiming exemption.

## Design Checklist

- [ ] Step-free route from street to all public/work areas
- [ ] Accessible entrance door (width, threshold, handle clearance)
- [ ] Lift if building >2 accessible floors
- [ ] At least one fully accessible WC per floor (public buildings)
- [ ] Accessible parking with transfer zone
- [ ] Tactile guidance (public buildings)
- [ ] Visual and tactile floor level indicators in lifts
- [ ] Contrast markings at glass doors and stairs
- [ ] Emergency evacuation plan for mobility-impaired users (refuge areas)
