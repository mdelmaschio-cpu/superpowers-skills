## SIA 102 — Architect's Mandate Structure

### Project Phases (Leistungsphasen)
1. **Strategische Planung** (SP) — Strategic planning
2. **Vorstudien** (VS) — Preliminary studies
3. **Projektierung** (PJ) — Project design (subdivided: Vorprojekt, Bauprojekt)
4. **Ausschreibung** (AS) — Tendering
5. **Realisierung** (RL) — Construction (Bauleitung)
6. **Bewirtschaftung** (BW) — Facility management

### Fee Calculation Basis
Fees calculated as percentage of construction cost (BKP 2) based on:
- Building category (1–5)
- Difficulty level (basic, standard, complex)
- Fee table in Appendix C of SIA 102

## SIA 118 — Construction Contract Key Points

- Applies to all construction work unless superseded by specific contract
- Contractor liability: material defects covered 5 years (CO art. 371)
- Payment: monthly partial payments against certified work
- Retention: typically 10% until acceptance, then 5% for defect period
- Works inspection (Abnahme): triggers start of defect period
- Dispute resolution: SIA arbitration or ordinary courts
