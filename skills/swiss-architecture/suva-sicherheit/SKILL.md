# /suva-sicherheit — Swiss Construction Site Safety

## Legal Framework

| Instrument | Content |
|-----------|---------|
| **ArG** (Arbeitsgesetz) | Federal law on worker protection |
| **UVG** (Unfallversicherungsgesetz) | Accident insurance — SUVA administers |
| **VUV** (Verordnung über die Unfallverhütung) | Accident prevention regulation |
| **BauAV** (Bauarbeitenverordnung) | Specific construction work ordinance — most relevant |
| **EKAS Richtlinie 6508** | Safety coordinator roles — **key document** |
| **SUVA publications** (Merkblätter) | Practical guidance per hazard type |

**SUVA** (Schweizerische Unfallversicherungsanstalt) is the federal accident insurance and safety enforcement authority for construction.

## EKAS Richtlinie 6508 — Safety Coordination

This directive, modeled on the EU Construction Sites Directive, defines **two mandatory coordinator roles** for construction projects above thresholds.

### When Coordination is Required

| Trigger | Obligation |
|---------|-----------|
| Project phase (design) | **SiKo-P** (Planungsphase coordinator) |
| Construction phase | **SiKo-B** (Bauphase coordinator) |
| **Multiple contractors** on site simultaneously | Both coordinators mandatory |
| Project > 500 person-days OR > 30 workers simultaneously | **Sicherheitsplan** mandatory |
| Particularly dangerous work (list in Anhang II BauAV) | Enhanced requirements |

### Particularly Dangerous Work (Anhang II BauAV)
- Deep excavations (> 2 m) without shoring
- Work near high-voltage lines
- Use of explosives
- Caisson/compressed air work
- Work with toxic substances
- Demolition of load-bearing elements
- Tunneling

### Who Can Be Safety Coordinator?

- **SiKo-P** (planning): architect, engineer, or specialist with SUVA-recognized safety training
- **SiKo-B** (construction): ideally the site supervisor (Bauleiter) — architect or specialized safety coordinator
- **Not**: the contractor performing the work (conflict of interest)

Recommended training: SUVA "Sicherheitskoordinator auf Baustellen" course

## Sicherheitsplan (Safety Plan)

Mandatory when project exceeds thresholds. Contents per EKAS 6508:

```
SICHERHEITSPLAN
Projekt:        [Name, address]
Bauherr:        [Client name]
SiKo-P:         [Name, qualification, contact]
SiKo-B:         [Name, qualification, contact]
Datum:          [Date]

1. PROJEKTBESCHREIBUNG
   Bauvolumen: [m³ / CHF]
   Schätzung Personentage: [number]
   Max. gleichzeitige Arbeitnehmer: [number]

2. GEFÄHRDUNGSANALYSE (Hazard Analysis)
   [List: hazard / affected parties / prevention measure / responsible party]
   - Absturzgefahr: [height, edge protection, scaffolding type]
   - Erdreich/Aushub: [depth, shoring type, groundwater]
   - Lärm/Vibration: [sources, protection measures]
   - Gefahrstoffe: [asbestos, lead survey before demolition]
   - Elektrizität: [temporary power supply, earthing]
   - Kranbetrieb: [crane position, swing zones, exclusion areas]

3. KOORDINATIONSMASSNAHMEN
   [How simultaneous contractors are coordinated]
   - Abgrenzung Arbeitsbereiche
   - Zugang und Wege auf Baustelle
   - Gemeinsame Einrichtungen (Sanitär, Lager)

4. BESONDERE MASSNAHMEN (Anhang II works)
   [Per dangerous work type: specific measures]

5. NOTFALLORGANISATION
   Notruf: 144 (Ambulanz), 118 (Feuerwehr), 117 (Polizei)
   Nächstes Spital: [Name, address, tel.]
   Ersthelfer auf Baustelle: [Name]
   Unfallmeldung SUVA: within 24h for serious accidents

6. UNTERSCHRIFTEN
   Bauherr: ___________  SiKo-P: ___________  SiKo-B: ___________
```

## Architect's Obligations and Liability

### As Project Architect (Bauleiter per SIA 102)
The architect acting as construction supervisor bears responsibility for:
- Appointing safety coordinators (or taking the role if qualified)
- Ensuring contractors comply with BauAV
- Stopping work if immediate danger exists (Arbeitsunterbrechungspflicht)
- Documenting safety checks in Bauleiterprotokoll

### Criminal Liability Risk
- Negligent failure to ensure site safety → **Strafgesetzbuch art. 117** (negligent homicide) or **art. 125** (negligent injury)
- Not a theoretical risk: Swiss prosecutors actively pursue construction site deaths
- **Document everything**: every safety check, every instruction to contractor

### Architect's Limits
- Cannot personally enforce safety inside contractor's work — contractor is responsible for their workers
- Can and must stop dangerous activities visible during site visits
- Must notify client if contractor persistently ignores safety requirements

## SUVA Inspections

- SUVA inspectors have authority to enter construction sites without notice
- Can issue stop-work orders immediately for serious violations
- Fines: up to CHF 10'000 per violation; repeat violations escalate
- **Common violations**: missing edge protection, unsecured excavations, no fall arrest PPE at height >2m, missing safety plan

## Asbestos (Asbest) — Special Protocol

**Always required before demolition or renovation of buildings built before 1990:**

1. **Asbest-Voruntersuchung** (pre-survey): certified specialist takes samples
2. **Lab analysis**: confirm presence/absence and type
3. If asbestos present: **certified asbestos removal contractor** (SUVA-accredited)
4. Personal air monitoring during removal
5. Disposal at licensed facility with documentation chain
6. **Clearance measurement** after removal before other trades enter

Architect's responsibility: commission survey, ensure only certified contractor used, document in Sicherheitsplan.

## Practical Checklist per Phase

### Design Phase (SiKo-P)
- [ ] Assess whether EKAS 6508 applies (person-days, simultaneous contractors)
- [ ] Appoint SiKo-P (self or specialist)
- [ ] Identify Anhang II dangerous work
- [ ] Draft Sicherheitsplan
- [ ] Check for asbestos in existing building
- [ ] Specify site safety measures in tender documents (Ausschreibung)

### Construction Phase (SiKo-B)
- [ ] Appoint SiKo-B before first contractor mobilizes
- [ ] Site safety briefing for all contractors at start
- [ ] Safety check at every site visit — document in Bauleiterprotokoll
- [ ] Update Sicherheitsplan when new contractors join
- [ ] Incident/near-miss log maintained
- [ ] SUVA accident report within 24h of serious accident
