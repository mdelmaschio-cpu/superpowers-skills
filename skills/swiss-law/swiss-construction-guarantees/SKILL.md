# /swiss-construction-guarantees — Swiss Construction Guarantees and Suretyship

## Legal Basis

- **OR art. 492–512**: Bürgschaft (suretyship / fideiussione)
- **OR art. 82–83**: Exceptio non adimpleti contractus (right to withhold performance)
- **SIA 118 art. 149–154**: Guarantees in construction contracts
- **SIA 102 §3.6**: Architect's obligation to require guarantees from contractors

---

## Overview: Types of Construction Guarantees

| Type | Swiss term | Trigger | Typical amount | Duration |
|------|-----------|---------|---------------|---------|
| Performance bond | Ausführungsgarantie | Contractor default | 10% of contract | Until acceptance |
| Payment guarantee (for client) | Zahlungsgarantie | Client non-payment | 100% of contract value | Until contract end |
| Defects guarantee | Gewährleistungsgarantie / Mängelgarantie | Defects in warranty period | 5–10% of contract | 2–5 years post-acceptance |
| Advance payment guarantee | Vorauszahlungsgarantie | Contractor fails to perform after advance | 100% of advance | Until advance earned |
| Retention substitute | Retentionsablösegarantie | Replaces cash retention | Equal to retention | Until defect period expires |

## 1. Bürgschaft (Suretyship — OR art. 492–512)

### Types

**Simple Bürgschaft (einfache Bürgschaft, OR art. 495)**
- Guarantor can require creditor to **first pursue the principal debtor** (Einrede der Vorausklage) before calling the guarantee
- Rarely used in construction — too slow in practice

**Solidary Bürgschaft (solidarische Bürgschaft, OR art. 496)**
- Guarantor is liable jointly and severally with the principal debtor
- Creditor can call directly on the guarantor without first pursuing the contractor
- Standard in construction bank guarantees

**Banking Guarantee vs. Bürgschaft**
In practice, Swiss banks issue **abstract guarantees on first demand** (Bankgarantie auf erstes Anfordern) — these are not governed by OR Bürgschaft rules but by general contract law and banking practice. They are:
- Payable immediately on written demand, without proof of underlying claim
- Independent of the underlying construction contract (abstract)
- More secure for the beneficiary than an ordinary Bürgschaft

## 2. SIA 118 Guarantee Regime (art. 149–154)

SIA 118 applies when expressly incorporated in the construction contract. Its guarantee provisions:

### Article 149 — Performance Guarantee (Ausführungsgarantie)
- **Amount**: typically 10% of contract price
- **Form**: bank guarantee or surety bond
- **Release**: at final acceptance (Abnahme) → replaced by defects guarantee
- **Call conditions**: contractor fails to complete, abandons work, enters insolvency

### Article 150 — Defects Guarantee (Gewährleistungsgarantie)
- **Amount**: 5% of final contract price (SIA 118 default)
- **Duration**: covers the 2-year SIA defect liability period (5 years for hidden defects under OR)
- **Release**: at expiry of defect liability period, provided no outstanding defects
- **Staged release**: first half at 2-year inspection, second half after all notified defects repaired

### Article 151 — Retention (Einbehalt)
Alternative to guarantee: client retains a percentage of each payment invoice:
- Standard: **5–10% of each invoice**, capped at 10% of contract total
- Client must release retention within 30 days of guarantee being provided
- **Retention substitute guarantee** (Retentionsablösegarantie): contractor provides bank guarantee to unlock cash retention → improves contractor cash flow

## 3. Drafting a Bank Guarantee Clause

### Key Elements to Specify in the Contract

```
Garantieverpflichtung:
Der Unternehmer verpflichtet sich, innert 10 Tagen nach Vertragsunterzeichnung eine 
unwiderrufliche, unbedingte Bankgarantie auf erstes schriftliches Anfordern 
(ohne Einreden aus dem Grundgeschäft) zu stellen:

a) Ausführungsgarantie: [10]% des Werkpreises (CHF [Betrag])
   Laufzeit: bis zur Abnahme gemäss SIA 118 art. 158
   
b) Gewährleistungsgarantie: [5]% des Endabrechnungsbetrages
   Laufzeit: [2] Jahre ab Abnahmedatum

Die Garantien sind von einer in der Schweiz zugelassenen Bank auszustellen.
Bei Nichtleistung der Garantie ist der Auftraggeber berechtigt, 
einen entsprechenden Betrag aus den Werklohnzahlungen einzubehalten.

(The contractor undertakes, within 10 days of contract signing, to provide an irrevocable, 
unconditional bank guarantee payable on first written demand 
[without defences from the underlying contract]: ...)
```

### Guarantee Text Template (Abstract First-Demand Guarantee)

```
BANKGARANTIE
Begünstigter: [Client name and address]
Auftraggeber/Antragsteller: [Contractor name]
Garantiebetrag: CHF [Amount]
Gültigkeitsdauer: bis [Date]
Garantiefall: [Project name, contract reference]

Die unterzeichnete Bank [Bank name] verpflichtet sich hiermit unwiderruflich 
und unbedingt, dem Begünstigten auf sein erstes schriftliches Zahlungsverlangen 
hin den Betrag von CHF [Amount] zu bezahlen, ohne Rücksicht auf Einwendungen 
aus dem Grundgeschäft.

Die Garantie erlischt mit Ablauf der Gültigkeitsdauer.
```

## 4. Calling a Guarantee

### When the Client Can Call
- Contractor in default (delay, abandonment, insolvency)
- Contractor refuses to repair notified defects within set deadline
- Contractor fails to complete work after termination notice
- During defect liability period: contractor ignores Mängelrüge and repair deadline

### Procedure for Calling
1. Send formal written notice (eingeschriebener Brief) to contractor demanding performance — set deadline (typically 10–20 working days)
2. If contractor fails to respond: send written demand to the bank guarantor
3. Demand must reference: guarantee number, amount called, project name
4. Bank must pay within 2–5 banking days (per guarantee terms)
5. Amounts received reduce outstanding claim; contractor retains right to contest in court afterward

### Contractor's Challenge of a Called Guarantee
An abstract first-demand guarantee is very difficult to contest: the contractor must prove the call was **manifestly abusive** (rechtsmissbräuchlich) — Swiss courts set a very high bar. A mere contractual dispute about whether defects exist is insufficient to block payment.

## 5. Release of Guarantees — Architect's Role

### Architect's Obligations (SIA 102 §3.6)
- **Before Abnahme**: confirm performance guarantee is in place before first payment
- **At Abnahme**: recommend transition from performance guarantee to defects guarantee
- **During defect period**: document all notified defects (Mängelrüge); advise client to maintain guarantee until all defects repaired
- **At expiry of defect period**: issue formal certificate of defect-free state (Mängelfreiheitsbescheinigung) → basis for releasing defects guarantee

### Release Checklist
- [ ] All notified defects repaired and confirmed in writing
- [ ] 2-year inspection protocol signed (or 5 years for hidden defects)
- [ ] No pending arbitration or court proceedings related to defects
- [ ] Final accounts (Schlussabrechnung) settled or in dispute resolution
- [ ] Client confirms in writing authorization to release guarantee

---

## 6. Insolvency of Contractor — Guarantee Strategy

If contractor enters insolvency (Konkurs) during works:
1. Call the performance guarantee **immediately** — before proceeds enter the bankruptcy estate
2. File claim in bankruptcy proceedings for any amounts exceeding the guarantee (Konkursanmeldung, SchKG)
3. Assess cost to complete: use guarantee funds + bankruptcy claim to commission replacement contractor
4. Document all additional costs for bankruptcy claim as privileged amounts if arising from employment (third-class creditor otherwise — recovery low)

**Priority in contractor bankruptcy**: architect's client (for guarantee proceeds already called) ranks above general creditors. But unsecured contract claims are third-class — recover little if insolvent estate is insufficient.

## Quick Reference — Guarantee Amounts (SIA 118 Defaults)

| Guarantee | Amount | When provided | When released |
|-----------|--------|--------------|--------------|
| Performance | 10% contract | Within 10 days of signing | At Abnahme |
| Defects | 5% final contract | At Abnahme (replaces above) | After defect period + repairs |
| Advance payment | 100% of advance | Before advance paid | As advance is earned off |
| Retention substitute | = retention held | When contractor requests | Same as retention release |
