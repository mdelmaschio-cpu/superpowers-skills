# /swiss-procurement-law — Swiss Public Procurement

## Legal Framework

| Instrument | Scope | Authority |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| **BöB** (Bundesgesetz über das öffentliche Beschaffungswesen) | Federal procurement | Federal level |
| **IVöB** (Interkantonale Vereinbarung über das öffentliche Beschaffungswesen, 2019) | Cantonal and municipal | All cantons (harmonized) |
| **WTO GPA** | International threshold | WTO member obligations |
| **Bilateral II (CH-EU)** | Cross-border access | EU operators in CH |

The **revised IVöB 2019** (in force in most cantons) aligns with BöB, creating a largely harmonized Swiss system.

## Procedures and Thresholds

### Thresholds (CHF, excl. VAT) — Goods and Services

| Procedure | Federal (BöB) | Cantonal (IVöB) |
|-----------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Einladungsverfahren** (Invitation) | < 150'000 | < 150'000 |
| **Selektives Verfahren** (Selective) | 150'000–8.7M | 150'000–8.7M |
| **Offenes Verfahren** (Open) | > 150'000 | > 150'000 |
| **WTO / International** | > 8.7M (goods/services) | > 8.7M |

### Thresholds — Construction Works

| Procedure | Federal | Cantonal |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Einladung | < 500'000 | < 500'000 |
| Offen / Selektiv | > 500'000 | > 500'000 |
| WTO International | > 10.8M | > 10.8M |

### Thresholds — Design Mandates (Architecture/Engineering)

| Procedure | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| Invitation | < 150'000 |
| Open/Selective | ≥ 150'000 |
| WTO | ≥ 250'000 |

*Above WTO threshold: SIMAP publication mandatory; EU firms may participate.*

## Procedures in Detail

### Offenes Verfahren (Open Procedure)
- Publication on **simap.ch** (Swiss public procurement portal)
- Any qualified provider may submit
- Mandatory for above-threshold contracts
- Evaluation criteria published in invitation documents

### Selektives Verfahren (Selective Procedure)
- Pre-qualification stage: providers submit qualifications
- Contracting authority selects shortlist (typically 3–8)
- Selected providers submit full offers

### Einladungsverfahren (Invitation Procedure)
- Below threshold; authority invites minimum 3 providers
- No publication required
- Faster, less formal

### Freihändiges Verfahren (Negotiated Procedure)
- Direct award; only allowed in exceptional cases:
  - Unique provider / monopoly
  - Extreme urgency
  - Very small contracts (typically < CHF 50'000)
  - Framework contract follow-on

## Award Criteria (Zuschlagskriterien)

Must be stated in advance. Cannot be changed post-submission.

**Price** is one criterion among several. Common criteria for architecture mandates:

| Criterion | Typical weight |
|-----------|---------------|
| Fee / price | 25–40% |
| Project understanding / concept | 20–30% |
| Team / references / experience | 15–25% |
| Methodology / working approach | 10–20% |
| Availability / capacity | 5–10% |

Award to **economically most advantageous offer** — not necessarily cheapest.

## Exclusion and Qualification Requirements

### Mandatory Exclusion Grounds
- Criminal conviction for corruption, money laundering, organized crime
- Tax or social security debts outstanding
- Insolvency or liquidation proceedings

### Typical Qualification Requirements (Eignungskriterien)
- Professional registration (REG A/B for architects in CH)
- Professional liability insurance (Berufshaftpflicht)
- Economic/financial capacity (turnover, equity)
- References: similar projects by type/value/date
- Workforce capacity

## Tender Process Timeline (Open Procedure)

1. **Publication** on simap.ch
2. **Submission period**: minimum 40 days (WTO threshold) / 20 days (below)
3. **Clarification questions**: deadline typically 2/3 through submission period
4. **Opening**: all offers opened simultaneously
5. **Evaluation**: against published criteria
6. **Award decision** (Zuschlagsverfügung): published on simap.ch
7. **Standstill period**: 20 days before contract signed (challenge window)
8. **Contract signature**

## Remedies — Challenging a Decision

- **Exclusion decision**: challenge to cantonal procurement review authority (Rekursinstanz)
- **Award decision**: challenge within **20 days** of publication
- Suspensive effect: challenge automatically suspends contract signing (in most cantons)
- Standard of review: procedural compliance + legality of criteria application
- **No damages** for lost profit if challenge succeeds — remedy is new procedure

## Practical Tips for Architects

- Register on **simap.ch** and set up alerts for relevant categories
- Monitor CPV (Common Procurement Vocabulary) codes: 71000000 (architectural services)
- Competition (SIA 142) counts as compliant procurement for design mandates
- Framework agreements: common for recurring small mandates (cleaning, maintenance, small works)
- **Debriefing right**: after unsuccessful bid, request written debriefing — legal right under IVöB
