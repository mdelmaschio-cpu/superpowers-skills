# /swiss-corporate-law — Swiss Corporate Law

## Legal Basis

- **OR/CO art. 620–763**: Aktiengesellschaft (SA/AG) — corporation
- **OR/CO art. 772–827**: GmbH/Sàrl — limited liability company
- **OR/CO art. 552–619**: Partnerships (collective, limited)
- **Revised OR**: in force 1 January 2023 — significant reforms

## Main Company Forms

| Form | Min. capital | Liability | Best for |
|------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **AG / SA** (Aktiengesellschaft) | CHF 100'000 (min. paid-in: CHF 50'000) | Limited to assets | Large firms, external investors, listed |
| **GmbH / Sàrl** | CHF 20'000 (fully paid-in) | Limited to assets | SMEs, professional practices |
| **Kollektivgesellschaft** (General partnership) | None | **Unlimited personal** | Small professional practices |
| **Kommanditgesellschaft** (Limited partnership) | None | Unlimited for general partner | Mixed investor structure |
| **Einzelunternehmen** (Sole trader) | None | **Unlimited personal** | Freelancers, individual architects |

## Aktiengesellschaft (AG/SA)

### Capital Structure
- Share capital (Aktienkapital): minimum CHF 100'000; at least CHF 50'000 paid-in at incorporation
- Shares: registered (Namenaktien) or bearer (Inhaberaktien — practically abolished post-2019 reforms)
- Nominal value: minimum CHF 0.01 per share (revised OR 2023)

### Governance Organs

| Organ | Composition | Function |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Generalversammlung (GV)** | All shareholders | Approve accounts, elect board, fundamental decisions |
| **Verwaltungsrat (VR)** | Min. 1 member | Strategy, supervision, non-delegable duties |
| **Geschäftsleitung** | Management team | Day-to-day operations (if delegated by VR) |
| **Revisionsstelle** | Auditor | Statutory audit (if required) |

### Non-Delegable Board Duties (OR art. 716a)
- Strategic direction
- Determination of organization
- Financial planning and controlling
- Appointment/removal of senior management
- Filing for insolvency if required

### Audit Requirements
- **Ordentliche Revision**: required if ≥2 of: 20+ employees (FTE), CHF 40M revenue, CHF 20M total assets
- **Eingeschränkte Revision**: smaller companies
- **Opting out**: possible for companies with ≤10 FTE if all shareholders agree

## GmbH/Sàrl

- Minimum capital: CHF 20'000, fully paid-in
- Shareholders (Gesellschafter) listed in commercial register — more transparent than AG
- Management (Geschäftsführer): minimum 1; majority must be Swiss residents (unless exemption)
- Transfer of shares: requires consent of shareholder meeting (more restrictive than AG)
- **Used by**: most small professional practices, architectural firms

## Director Liability (Verwaltungsrat/Geschäftsführer)

### Standard of Care
- Act with diligence of a "careful businessman in similar situation"
- Duty of loyalty: no conflicts of interest without disclosure
- Business judgment rule: courts defer to informed, good-faith decisions

### Personal Liability Triggers (OR art. 754)
- Intentional or negligent breach of duties
- **Insolvency**: failure to file timely (Überschuldungsanzeige OR art. 725a)
- **Capital loss** (Kapitalverlust, OR art. 725): must call GV, take measures
- False information in commercial register
- Social security arrears: personal liability for officers (AHVG art. 52)

### Revised OR 2023 — Key Changes
- Lower share nominal value (CHF 0.01)
- Capital band (Kapitalband): board may vary capital within 50% range without GV approval
- Enhanced shareholder rights
- Stricter requirements for bearer shares (effectively eliminated)
- New insolvency warning system (art. 725–725b)

## Architecture Firm Structure in Switzerland

| Structure | Pros | Cons |
|-----------|------|------|
| **Einzelunternehmen** | Simple, no capital required | Unlimited personal liability |
| **Kollektivgesellschaft** (partners) | Flexible, simple | Unlimited personal liability |
| **GmbH** | Limited liability, low capital | CHF 20'000 locked in; listed in register |
| **AG** | Full limited liability, flexible shares | CHF 100'000 capital; more governance |

**Most common for Swiss architectural practices**: GmbH (medium firms) or Kollektivgesellschaft (small partnerships)

## Cantonal Commercial Register

- All AG and GmbH must register (Handelsregister/Registre du commerce)
- Registration: online at zefix.ch
- Annual reporting: accounts filed (if audit required)
- Changes (address, management): 30-day notification obligation
