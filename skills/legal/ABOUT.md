# Legal Skills

Skills for Swiss legal work: statutory research grounded in the Swiss Code of Obligations (CO/SR 220), contract analysis and drafting, legal opinions, case-law research, and due diligence.

## Skills

- `swiss-code-obligations` — Search and cite the Swiss Code of Obligations (Codice delle obbligazioni, CO; RS/SR 220), Italian text, sourced from a bundled Fedlex PDF.
- `analisi-contratto-co` — Analyze an existing contract against CO provisions.
- `redazione-clausole-co` — Draft contract clauses compliant with the CO.
- `parere-legale` — Structure and write a formal Swiss legal opinion.
- `ricerca-giurisprudenza-svizzera` — Research Swiss case law (BGE/ATF).
- `due-diligence-legale-svizzera` — Run legal due diligence under Swiss law.
